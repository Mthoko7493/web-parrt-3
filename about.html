Our Story

Cane Villa Cabs And Tours is a mid-sized transportation and logistics company based in Johannesburg, specializing in both passenger and goods transport. Established in 2012, we have grown into a reliable provider with a strong focus on punctuality, safety, and customer satisfaction.

To deliver efficient, affordable, reliable, and sustainable transport solutions that connect people and businesses seamlessly.

To become a leading transportation provider known for innovation, reliability, and eco-conscious logistics.

Our Services Overview

Daily Transport Solutions

We provide reliable daily transport for individuals commuting to work, appointments, or personal engagements. Our flexible scheduling ensures you reach your destination on time, every time.

Corporate Transport Services

Professional transportation solutions for business clients, including executive transfers, client pickups, and employee shuttle services. We understand the importance of punctuality in business.

Event Transport Solutions

Comprehensive transport solutions for events, conferences, weddings, and special occasions. We ensure your guests arrive comfortably and on schedule.

Tourist Transport Partnerships

Collaboration with travel agencies to provide seamless tourist transport experiences, including airport transfers, city tours, and intercity travel.
